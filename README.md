#  Mobile Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing mobile sales data across cities, brands, and time periods to uncover key business insights.

## Key Features

* KPI Metrics: Sales, Quantity, Transactions, Average
* City-wise sales (map visualization)
* Monthly & daily sales trends
* Sales by mobile models & brands
* Payment method distribution
* Customer ratings analysis
* Interactive filters (Brand, Model, Time)

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel 

## Key Insights

* Peak sales observed in March and July
* UPI & Debit Cards are most used
* Top brands dominate overall sales
* Majority ratings are 4–5

documentation
## Business Use Case

This dashboard can help:
* Businesses track product performance
* Sales teams identify high-revenue locations
* Marketing teams plan campaigns
* Companies improve customer satisfaction
